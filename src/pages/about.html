About Naylor Homes

Naylor Homes is a privately owned rental business operating across Yorkshire and Lancashire.

Who we are

We manage a growing local portfolio of houses and high-specification HMOs, all run to a consistent standard.

While we operate at scale, our approach remains hands-on. We believe tenants should know who they are renting from and how their home will be looked after.

We focus on straightforward communication, reliable management, and homes that are maintained consistently over the long term.

Our priority is stable, high-quality living for people who want certainty and professionalism from their landlord.

What we specialise in

Our core lettings focus is clear and intentional.

Family homes for professionals

Well-maintained houses designed for long-term tenants who value reliable standards and responsive management.

High-end shared houses

High-specification HMOs with en-suite rooms, created for professionals seeking quality shared living.

Long-term tenancy approach

We do not let to students or operate short-term accommodation. Our model is built around stable, long-term homes.
